# Citation Generator & Manager
### Created by Liby

A **Windows desktop app** AND a **mobile web app (PWA)** for generating and managing academic citations in **APA**, **MLA**, **Chicago**, **ACM**, **IEEE**, and **Vancouver** formats — with auto-fill from DOI/URL/ISBN, a built-in **collaborative editor** with smart in-text citations, BibTeX export, and **Butter**, your AI citation assistant.

## Features

### Citation Formats
- **APA 7th Edition**, **MLA 9th Edition**, **Chicago (Author-Date)**, **ACM**, **IEEE**, and **Vancouver** citation styles
- **BibTeX export** — ready for LaTeX / Overleaf

### Auto-fill from Identifier ✨
- Paste a **DOI** (e.g. `https://doi.org/10.1000/xyz`) → fetches full metadata from CrossRef
- Paste a **URL** → scrapes title, site name, description, and publish date
- Paste an **ISBN** → fetches book info from Open Library
- Click **Lookup** or paste and it auto-triggers

### Citation Form
- **Dynamic author form** — Add multiple contributors with Surname, First Name, and M.I.
- **Source types** — Journal, Book, Website, Conference Paper
- **Abstract / Notes field** — Attach notes or paste the paper abstract
- **PDF attachment** — Link a local PDF file (desktop app only)

### Collaborative Editor (Desktop App)
- **Rich-text editor** — Write your paper with full formatting (bold, italic, headings, lists)
- **Smart in-text citations** — Select text and insert citations that land at sentence boundaries
- **6 citation styles** — Switch between APA, MLA, Chicago, ACM, IEEE, Vancouver on the fly
- **Toggleable highlights** — Show or hide highlighted citation passages
- **PDF viewer** — View attached reference PDFs side by side with your document
- **Export to .docx** — Export your entire document with formatted citations to Word

### Organization & Export
- **Folder organization** — Group citations by project or subject
- **Alphabetical sorting** — Citations sorted A–Z with quick letter navigation
- **Search** — Filter citations by text or notes
- **Export** — PDF, Word (.docx or .doc), or .bib file

### 🧈 Butter — AI Citation Assistant
- Ask Butter about APA, MLA, Chicago, ACM, IEEE, Vancouver formats and rules
- Get auto-fill tips, BibTeX/Overleaf help, and citation checking
- Typing indicator and friendly personality
- Available in both the desktop app and web app

## How to Use

### Citation Generator
1. Select **Source Type** and **Citation Format**
2. Paste a **DOI / URL / ISBN** and click **Lookup** to auto-fill, OR fill in fields manually
3. Click **Generate Citation**
4. Click **Add to List** to save it
5. Export your full reference list as PDF, Word, or BibTeX

### Collaborative Editor (Desktop)
1. Open the **Writer** window from the app menu
2. Create or open a document
3. Write your paper in the rich-text editor
4. Select text and click **Cite** to attach a reference — the in-text citation is inserted automatically
5. Use the style dropdown to switch citation formats — all citations re-format instantly
6. Click **Export** to download your document as .docx
